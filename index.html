    How To Use:
  1. Filling out gender and caste is mandatory.
  2. You can leave all the other fields empty (except gender and caste) to list out everything.
  3. You can fill in inst code and leave out rest of fields (except gender and caste) to output everybranch in that college.
  4. range_min and range_max is where you specify the cutoff range in which you want to list out the cutoffs.
  5. You can fill all the desired branches you want in the branch_code field (use commas "," to separate them (cse,inf,ece)) and submit it.
  6. If you have any doubts in branch codes or dist codes or inst codes you can check it out in Codes_and_Names which is at the Top.
  7. Use a computer for better view.
    Common Mistakes:
  1. Not opting gender and caste.
  2. Filling inst_code and dist_code which don't match.
  3. Filling multiple caste's.
